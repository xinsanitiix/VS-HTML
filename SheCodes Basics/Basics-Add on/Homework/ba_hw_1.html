What is Yoga?

Yoga, a Hindu spiritual and ascetic discipline, combines physical postures, and meditation. Widely practiced for health and relaxation breath control, simple meditation, and the adoption of specific bodily postures aids in aligning the body, mind, and spirit.

Remember to breathe deeply and enjoy the practice!
